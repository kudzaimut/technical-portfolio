Who Am I And What Am I Up To?

Hi, and welcome to my page that will give a brief window into my life and some of the exciting things I have done so far. My name is Kudzai Mutembwa and I am an aspiring product manager with a background in Economics, Public Policy and now technology (via Computer Science). In the first half of 2022, I completed a 6-month product management internship at iRobot, where I leveraged my experience and knowledge to advocate for iRobot users in all aspects of the product lifecycle.

I completed my undergraduate degree in International Economics, Management and Finance from Bocconi University in Milano, Italy and then moved to Boston, MA for grad school, first beginning with a degree in International Affairs, and now currently in Computer Science at Northeastern University. Yes, as you can tell, I love an adventure, and comfort zones are non-existent to me!

I hope to solve user pain-points and build the most effective and efficient strategies through:
- User experience and product research
- Data gathering and analysis
- Defining product roadmaps
- Engaging with partners internally and externally
- HAVING FUN DOING IT ALL!

*If you're looking for me, you will find me walking around Boston listening to my favorite Playlist. Or is it a podcast? Maybe a bit of both!

I worked in a group of 3 to create an app from ideation to execution using all tenets of product design, user research, product management and human computer interaction. The app was designed to allow users to carry a digital copy of their COVID vaccination cards, upload test results, schedule tests, as well as vaccine appointments. MassPass Prototype
