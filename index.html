Who Am I And What Am I Up To?

Hi, and welcome to my page that will give a brief window into my life and some of the exciting things I have done so far. My name is Kudzai Mutembwa and I am an aspiring user experience researcher and product manager with a background in Economics, Public Policy and now technology (via Computer Science). In the first half of 2022, I completed a 6-month product management internship at iRobot, where I leveraged my experience and knowledge to advocate for iRobot users in all aspects of the product lifecycle.

I completed my undergraduate degree in International Economics, Management and Finance from Bocconi University in Milano, Italy and then moved to Boston, MA for grad school, first beginning with a degree in International Affairs, and now Computer Science at Northeastern University. Finally, I just completed a graduate certificate in agile project management this Spring 2024! Yes, as you can tell, I love an adventure, and comfort zones are non-existent to me!

I hope to solve user pain-points and build the most effective and efficient strategies through:
- User experience and product research
- Data gathering and analysis
- Defining product roadmaps
- Engaging with partners internally and externally
- HAVING FUN DOING IT ALL!

*If you're looking for me, you will find me walking around Boston listening to my favorite Playlist. Or is it a podcast? Maybe a bit of both!

Projects

I have been fortunate enough to work on some very exciting projects in my brief time working in technology. From UX research and ideating for apps to researching the efficacy of AI moderation in Virtual Reality, these projects have allowed me to gain invaluable UX and software skills across the technology space.

MassPass App

I worked in a group of 3 to create an app from ideation to execution using all tenets of product design, user research, product management and human computer interaction. The app was designed to allow users to carry a digital copy of their COVID vaccination cards, upload test results, schedule tests, as well as vaccine appointments. Link: MassPass Prototype

Audio Visual

Technical project that focused on creating a business case for a client's non-profit yoga business. Developed recommendations based on the business needs of the client and presented data driven solutions. Created a plan for implementing recommendations and followed up with client to ensure smooth adoption. Link: Social Media PowerPoint

AI Moderation in VR

I worked in a group of 3 to understand from a UX perspective the attitudes towards an increased use AI in moderating content that is viewed on VR settings. We used focus groups and interviews to understand VR users' understanding of AI within content moderation. I juxtaposed that information to current literature, whilst also highlighting ways in which AI can successfully be utilized.
